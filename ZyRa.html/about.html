Zyra is India's leading omnichannel beauty, wellness, and fashion retailer, founded by Falguni Nayar in 2012, offering a vast range of domestic and international brands online and in physical stores, known for its content-driven approach, authenticity, and as a pioneer female-led unicorn startup. It provides makeup, skincare, haircare, fragrances, and more, alongside expert advice, transforming India's beauty retail with a tech-forward, consumer-centric model. Key Aspects of Nykaa: Founding & Vision: Started by Falguni Nayar, it aimed to make beauty accessible and mainstream in India, disrupting the market with quality products and digital expertise. Product Range: Sells a huge selection of beauty (makeup, skincare, hair care), wellness, personal care, and fashion items from global and local brands. Omnichannel Presence: Operates through its popular website, mobile apps, and over 150 physical stores, blending online and offline experiences. Content & Community: Offers expert videos, blogs, and virtual makeover tools to engage customers and build communities. Authenticity & Quality: Emphasizes ensuring product authenticity and high quality through strict checks. Growth & Recognition: Became India's first woman-led unicorn startup (valued at over $1 billion) in 2020 and continues to be a major player in the growing Indian beauty market. In essence, Nykaa is a digitally native consumer technology platform that brings inspiration and joy through a curated lifestyle retail experience, focusing on beauty, personal care, and fashion for modern Indian consumers.
